# 48-Hour Plan: Make the First $500

This is not a guaranteed-income plan. It is the highest-probability path from zero to a paid pilot: sell a simple service before building a full SaaS.

## Offer

**Productized service:** AI receptionist and lead follow-up setup for local service businesses.

**Price:** $500 for a 48-hour pilot. If the buyer asks for more support, quote $900 setup plus $199/month maintenance.

**Best niches to contact first:**

- HVAC and plumbing companies
- Dental clinics and med spas
- Auto repair shops
- Immigration, personal injury, and family law firms
- Roofing, landscaping, pest control, and garage door companies

Why these niches: missed calls and slow replies cost real money, the owner understands lost appointments, and a single recovered job can justify a $500 pilot.

## What You Actually Deliver

Use simple tools first. Do not overbuild.

- A website chat intake flow or form that asks qualifying questions.
- A business FAQ and lead script based on the client's website.
- Email or SMS-ready lead summaries for the owner.
- A post-job review request template.
- A 20-minute handoff call or Loom-style walkthrough.

## Day 1

1. Pick one niche and one city.
2. Build a list of 100 businesses from Google Maps, Yelp, chamber directories, or local search.
3. Prioritize businesses with weak websites, no chat, slow-looking contact forms, few recent reviews, or obvious emergency/appointment services.
4. Send 80 short emails.
5. Send 20 website contact-form messages.
6. Call 10 of the warmest-looking businesses and use the short script below.

## Day 2

1. Follow up with everyone who opened, replied, or has a visible owner email.
2. Offer to create a free 3-question mockup for their business.
3. Close one pilot at $500 using Stripe, PayPal, Wise, or invoice.
4. Deliver within 48 hours using the scope above.

## Website Contact Form Message

Hi, I help local service businesses recover missed leads with a small AI receptionist setup. It answers basic questions, captures job details, and sends the owner a qualified lead summary. I can install a 48-hour pilot for $500. Who handles new customer inquiries for your team?

## Phone Script

Hi, is this the owner or manager? I will be brief.

I help local service businesses stop losing leads when calls or website messages are missed. I install a simple AI receptionist flow that qualifies the customer and sends you a clean summary.

For businesses like yours, one recovered job can often cover the pilot. I am offering one 48-hour setup this week for $500. Can I send you a tiny mockup based on your website?

## Discovery Questions

- What are the top 5 questions new customers ask?
- Which services are most profitable?
- What location or ZIP codes do you serve?
- What makes a lead urgent?
- Where should new lead summaries go?
- Do you want appointment booking, call-back requests, or quote requests first?

## Build Checklist After Payment

- Read the client's website and Google Business Profile.
- Write a 10-question intake script.
- Write FAQ answers in the business owner's tone.
- Create a lead summary format.
- Create the review request message.
- Test the flow with 5 fake leads.
- Record or write a short handoff guide.

## Risk Controls

- Do not promise guaranteed revenue.
- Do not claim the AI replaces staff.
- Do not handle medical, legal, or financial advice directly; route sensitive questions to the business.
- Keep the first pilot narrow: one service line, one intake path, one destination for leads.
- Collect payment before building custom work.

## Close Line

The pilot is $500, installed in 48 hours, and focused on one thing: making sure new leads get captured and routed to you quickly. Should I send the invoice?

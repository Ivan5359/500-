# Daily Operator Playbook

## Goal

Book one paid `Quote Recovery Sprint` at `$500`, then convert successful clients into `$299-799/month` follow-up retainers.

## Daily Numbers

- Find 50 contractors.
- Verify 25 websites.
- Send 40 approved messages.
- Make 10 short calls.
- Book 3 discovery calls.
- Close 1 sprint every 1-3 days.

## Best Leads

Prioritize businesses where:

- site has `free estimate`, `request quote`, `schedule consultation`;
- job value is high: roofing, HVAC, flooring, remodeling, fencing;
- owner-operated or small team;
- they show recent work photos/projects;
- they have a phone number and email/form;
- they probably have a CRM or spreadsheet of old quotes.

Avoid:

- franchises with corporate systems;
- emergency-only businesses;
- very tiny businesses with no website/contact;
- medical, legal, finance for this offer;
- businesses where SMS consent is unclear unless client sends messages manually.

## First Message

```text
Hi, quick idea for {{business}}.

Do you have old estimates or leads where the homeowner went quiet after comparing options?

I help {{niche}}s run a 48-hour quote recovery sprint: you send 20-50 past quotes/leads, I segment them, write approved follow-ups, and set up a simple callback tracking board.

Fixed $500 for the first sprint. If one old estimate turns back into a job, it can pay for itself.

Want me to send the one-page workflow?
```

## When They Reply

```text
Great. The workflow is simple:

1. You send 20-50 old estimates/no-response leads.
2. I segment them into Hot/Warm/Cold.
3. I write approved follow-up messages.
4. We track replies and callbacks in a simple board.
5. You or your team handles the actual calls/bookings.

The first sprint is $500 and takes 48 hours after I receive the list.
```

## Close

```text
If you want, I can send the invoice and onboarding checklist now.
Once paid, I only need the old quote list and your approval rules.
```

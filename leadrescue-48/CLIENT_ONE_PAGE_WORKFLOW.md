# Quote Recovery Sprint

## What This Is

A 48-hour campaign to recover old estimates and no-response leads that already cost your business time or ad spend.

## Who It Is For

Contractors who send estimates and have leads that go quiet after comparing options:

- roofing;
- HVAC;
- painting;
- flooring;
- fencing;
- landscaping;
- remodeling;
- plumbing with high-value jobs.

## What You Send

A CSV, spreadsheet, or CRM export with 20-50 old estimates/leads:

- name;
- phone/email;
- service requested;
- estimate date;
- estimate value;
- current status;
- any notes from the conversation.

## What I Build

1. Lead segmentation: `Hot`, `Warm`, `Cold`.
2. Approved follow-up messages for each segment.
3. A callback tracking board.
4. A simple status workflow:
   - Needs follow-up
   - Sent
   - Replied
   - Callback booked
   - Won
   - Lost
5. A short report showing who should be contacted next.

## Timeline

Day 1:

- Review your old quotes/leads.
- Segment the list.
- Draft follow-up messages.
- Send for approval.

Day 2:

- Finalize approved messages.
- Build callback board.
- Prepare sending queue.
- Hand off next actions.

## Price

`$500` fixed for the first sprint.

No guaranteed sales. The value is in giving old estimates a structured second chance and making sure no-response leads do not stay forgotten.
